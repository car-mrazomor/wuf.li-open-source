How can I connect my mail client?

Use an Exchange option, the server name is mail.wuf.li and username is both username and domain

How can I reset password or I forgot password?

You will need to contact me, check contact page.

I can not login into account!

Either server is down or you are banned for any reason down below.

How to not get banned

You may be banned for the following reasons:

  • Sending spam
  • Bulk registration
  • Using your e-mail to abuse other services
  • Conducting any activity that breaks the laws in which Wuf is governed

I am not getting instructions for setting domain up!

Make sure to check spam/junk mailboxes and if it's not there, please contact me.

My domain it's not shown there!

Either your domain is banned or something went wrong. In both cases you can contact me.

I can not recieve or send emails from domain name I added!

Make sure to setup DNS records as you get email from adding domain.
